# RUBRIC — the AI-native acceptance gate

*The "tests" of this SDLC loop. A pattern does not enter the evolved
Commons until it passes here. This gate is what makes "AI-native" an
earned label, not a claimed one (the 30-day signal: "You're Not AI
Native" — most products claim it by bolting chat on; ours must prove it).*

## The craft bar (carried forward — necessary, not sufficient)

Every pattern still meets the foundry's original bar: portfolio-grade,
not demo-grade. Type leads, the arrival is choreographed, chrome
whispers. A pattern that fails craft fails regardless of AI-native score.

## The AI-native axis (the new gate — the evolution)

Each pattern is scored honestly against three axes. **Decoration is
allowed to exist; it is just labeled decoration.** The lie we refuse is
calling a pretty-graph pattern "AI-native."

1. **commitment-bearing** (was "register" — reconciled 2026-06-16, see
   RECONCILIATION.md) — does the pattern distinguish COMMITMENT on the
   generative-typing lifecycle (propose -> admit): an admitted RECORD
   (ink) vs a PROPOSED / SIMULATED draft (pencil)? Bound to
   display-system's lifecycle + strata, NOT a free four-enum. RUNTIME is
   NOT on this axis — "happening / parked" is process state (anima-tempo +
   activation + async edge), scored under agent-drivable/provenance or
   left off, never as a register. `none | partial | full`

2. **provenance-bearing** — does every element carry a *traceable route
   to its source*? Click anything → its backing record / file:line / log
   substring. The holographic rule: no projection without a path home.
   `none | partial | full`

3. **agent-drivable** — can a model compose or drive this surface, and is
   there an API / typed-spec equivalent to the gesture (not browser-only)?
   Does authoring go through a schema-validated spec before it renders?
   `none | partial | full`

## Verdict (derived, not invented)

- **ai-native** — scores `full`/`partial` on ≥2 axes AND survives the
  "earn-it" question: *is the AI substantive to the pattern, or bolted on?*
- **truth-capable** — scores on exactly 1 axis; a craft pattern that can
  carry truth with work.
- **decoration** — scores `none` on all three. Kept for craft, labeled
  honestly. (Most of the original 26 will land here — that is the finding,
  not a failure.)

## New-pattern bar (families 8/9/10)

A newly authored pattern MUST verdict **ai-native** (≥2 axes) to enter.
It must carry: a concrete `intent`, a real `use_when`, at least one
*grounded* exemplar (a live surface or `foundry:`/`causality:` ref — never
a guessed 2026 landmark stated as fact), and a `non_example` (what it is
NOT — the grip discipline).

## The refusal (teeth)

A pattern that claims an AI-native verdict its axis scores do not support
is **sent back with the divergence named** — the same refusal
`causality/term_economy.py` makes on a ghost citation. No pattern mints
itself; bdo's praise is the only promotion past the gate (D-4).
