# Panel report — the ontum corpus, its derived grammar, and the first ontum

**Status:** PROPOSED · owner **bdo** · authored by the review, his to steer (D-4). Nothing minted.
**Provenance:** a cross-model review over the envoy package plus the live `ontum` (~865 files) and
`gallery` (~109 files) corpora, with a nine-agent derivation workflow (`wf_22d65bda-585`) folded in.
Every load-bearing claim is cited `path:line` and re-verified by grep against the extracted corpus.
**One line:** the corpus's working grammar is five operations; the first ontum is not a filled schema
but a *cited crossing*; the buildable next step is the consequence throat; the cube is a proposed
fabric lens, not the harness skeleton.
**Correction (companion, this session):** the five are not the floor — they are the basis *emitted by the
membrane under work-accountability pressure*. The deeper part is the **generalized seam / membrane** that
cuts slots into a reality under pressure; basis-generation sits *before* the throat. See §4·5 below and the
companion [generative-basis-membrane.proposal.md](generative-basis-membrane.proposal.md) + `loop/basis.py`.

## 1. Question and method

The ask was field linguistics, not code review: derive the corpus's *generative grammar* from
model-free consequence-mapping, and from it type "the first real ontum" — while saying plainly what is
working machinery and what is a pretty word with no machinery behind it.

The review held to a few standing rules. Claims resolve to `path:line` or they are ghosts. Terms carry
the corpus's own evidence grades (`minted-runtime / minted-doctrine / projected / proposed / poetic /
orphaned / ghost`) **and** a load class (`spine / subsystem / eval-instrument / display / vault`) —
because "enforced" is not "enforced *in the spine*." A primitive is primary only if removing it changes
what the system can admit, refuse, route, witness, or make durable. Grammar is counted as *roles under
divergent words*, not shared vocabulary. And the panel trusts the grep over its own consensus —
including a co-reviewer's report of a grep until it is re-run.

The single seam held open throughout: **unit-of-work vs. unit-of-meaning.** "atom" is a unit of work;
"the first ontum" is intended as a unit of meaning. The corpus is overwhelmingly the harness describing
itself, so these may be two types it has been blurring. The report refuses to assert `ontum = atom`.

## 3. The derived grammar

The basis is **five operations**, not the workflow's six — `POSTURE` reduces (finding I), the same way
the panel's own rule rejected `witness`. Each primary has a refusal tooth, re-verified at the
enforcement seat.

| primary | operation | refusal tooth | citation |
|---|---|---|---|
| **APPEND** | the one irreversible write to the truth plane | a torn-tail line is dropped on read — "it never happened" | `reconcile.py:82-86` |
| **FOLD** | state (and setter-less force) re-derived by replay, never cached | "the state of the world is this fold over log/ — never the cache" | `reconcile.py:4-5,130` |
| **HASH-IDENTITY** | content *is* identity; idempotence by `(node, hash)` | same `(node,hash)` is a no-op; changed bytes mint new identity | `reconcile.py:74,146-149` |
| **CITE** | a claim counts only if its citation resolves | unresolved backing is "REFUSED and listed as a gap — the ghost refusal" | `consequence_graph.py:36-37`; `field.py:316-393` |
| **VERDICT** | a bounded, independent judgment at a named seam | verdict must come from the seam's terminal set; "a node may not judge an event it announced (D-2)" | `node.py:5-9,102` |

**Mixing rule.** Every operational term is an ordered subset of the five along the path a fact travels:
`identity(HASH) → written(APPEND) → read(FOLD) → advanced(VERDICT) → admissible-iff(CITE) resolves`.
A term touching fewer is partial; touching none, poetic. Worked: `atom = VERDICT-walk ∘ APPEND ∘ HASH`;
`gateway/crossing = VERDICT(default-deny) ∘ APPEND over POSTURE(registration)`; `receipt = APPEND
bearing a VERDICT, CITE-able`; `ghost = ¬CITE`; `witness = APPEND made observable by FOLD`;
`POSTURE = FOLD over APPENDed governance admissions` — the last three decompose, so none is a primary.
The generative space is `{ordered subsets of the five}`: a fact travelling a new path is a new ontum
the system never pre-authored, discovered by replay, not fitted.

**The SPLIT.** Three differently-shaped structures, not two triples — so cardinality cannot unify them:
the cube coordinate (3 axes `{−,0,+}`, `eval-instrument`/`vault`), canvas known-depth
(`fundamental/derived/learned`, `display`), and language strata (`syntax/semantics/semiotics/pragmatics`,
4 layers, `declared`). Ruling: **SPLIT**. The live object's real axes are not `x/y/z` but
`known-depth × advance × standing` — and even `standing` decomposes (it is `POSTURE`), so this 3D is a
practical projection for navigating a work-traveler, not three primaries.

**The self-test.** A candidate primary with no resolving machinery must be refused — `CITE` turned on
the derivation. `POSTURE` is the live proof the gate is not yet closed: it passed the nine-agent panel
as a sixth primary, and only the re-grep demoted it. Any extracted derivation skill is not real until
it can self-demote `POSTURE` without a human catching it.

## 4. The first object

The generated typed object, named honestly **work-ontum.v0** because it is built on the work-atom while
the meaning seam stays open: *a fact travelling a path through the five primaries, observable only as a
`FOLD`* — born with a hash identity, never stored, re-derived each pass. Field tiers, kept honest:

- **real:** identity · provenance · incidence · advance_state · standing · consequence_marks
- **declared:** known_depth · position · dimension · anima.strength/tempo
- **poetic:** anima.pulse/rhythm/breath · territory

The fabric ontum — a *unit of meaning* — does not yet exist on disk (finding K). The corpus has built a
turnstile that could judge one, not the occupant. So the report's central honest statement: the harness
grammar is real and derived; the first *meaning* ontum is still open.

## 4·5. The membrane — the basis is pressure-relative (correction, supersedes the "five is the floor" reading)

The five-primary basis is not ontum's metaphysical floor. It is the **fitted gamut emitted by the membrane
under work-accountability pressure** — ontum's "RGB", the way B&W/grayscale/RGB/CMYK/PANTONE are different
spanning sets the eye's membrane emits under different pressures. The deeper part is the **generalized
seam**: the membrane that cuts slots into an undifferentiated reality (the all-axes-open prior, ⊘) under
pressure. A **slot** is a pressure-shaped distinction with a refusal-tooth. Basis changes (SPLIT · MERGE ·
ADD · DROP) are legal only when they preserve **coverage** and **refusal** (the gamut law). The throat
(§5) then tests whether an occupant can legally *cross* one of those slots — so it is **downstream** of the
membrane, not the floor.

The corrected stack (basis-generation moved before the throat):

```
⊘ / pressure        the undifferentiated prior the membrane cuts into
  → MEMBRANE        the generalized seam: VERDICT decides an axis, APPEND collapses 0→±, under a pressure
  → BASIS           the slots = the decided axes (five under accountability; +POSTURE under governance; …)
  → THROAT          tests one occupant's crossing of a slot (refuses ghost / unobservable / malformed)
  → FABRIC          meaning travelers inhabit slots natively
  → SHARED EXP.     humans/models speak and act over the fabric
```

This dissolves the report's one hidden flaw — treating the five as the derived grammar basis full-stop.
True, but only *at the accountability pressure setting*. `POSTURE`'s demotion (§3) is then not a final
status but a **legal `DROP` under accountability**, reversible to a legal `ADD` under governance — computed,
not asserted, by `loop/basis.py` (its §10 self-test admits both and refuses the fakes; run and passing this
session). The companion proposal carries the full algebra, the slot lifecycle, and the membrane reading;
this section is the load-bearing correction it makes to this report.

## 5. The buildable v1 — the consequence throat

Not the full consequence compiler (`situation + corpus + state → inferred reality`); that is too wide to
build first. The throat is the compiler's load-bearing inner step — where a candidate occupant becomes
accountable — and the build order is **immune system → throat → orchestration**.

It is *assembly of minted parts*, not invention: `field.route_audit` (ghost gate, `field.py:316-393`),
`observe.gate`/`act_fence.evaluate` (observable-act gate, `observe.py:136-157`, `act_fence.py:220-248`),
`relation_ledger.validate`/`fold` (`relation_ledger.py:158,269-288`). Two immune laws hold in code: *no
unbacked occupant may act as fact*; *no unobservable act may run as consequence.*

**Flow:** `situation → infer one occupant → ghost-gate (model-only if ghost) → relation_claim →
act_fence.evaluate (observe-first) → validate claim+receipt → fold → bucket`.

**Pass bar — scored, not incomplete.** The throat admits only `{PREDICTIVE, TRIVIAL}` — a *scored*
crossing where claim and receipt met. `{UNTESTED, UNCLAIMED}` are honest but incomplete (no crossing
completed). It grades whether claim and receipt met and were scored, not whether the prediction was
right — so `TRIVIAL` (refuted) is consequence truth, which guards against success theater.

**Acceptance — proven by refusal, not admission.** Given situation-zero, the throat must (1) refuse a
ghost subject, (2) `halt` an unobservable act, (3) validate one claim+receipt, (4) fold to
`PREDICTIVE`/`TRIVIAL`, (5) leave the trace — *and* a deliberately malformed input must trip gates 1–2.
An immune system is real only when it rejects.

**Risk:** the parallel truth store — a new kernel that subsumes these subsystems on paper and is
imported by none (the cube's failure, repeated). The implementation must retrofit one existing minted
record as occupant-zero, not land beside the subsystems, and must not depend on a cube coordinate.

## 6. The cube — a proposed fabric lens (not the skeleton)

The cube failed as the harness skeleton (findings A, D) and is decoupled from the throat. It re-enters
under a narrower, stronger claim: a **prior→posterior lattice for meaning decidedness**, `proposed`, not
minted. The reading is epistemic, not spatial — `0` = opened/undecided/prior, `±` = decided/posterior,
dimension = remaining uncertainty; the center `(0,0,0)` is pure prior (where `strata.md` places
pragmatics), a corner is fully decided (syntax). The five primaries map as *operators*, not stored
objects: `HASH` = traveler identity, `FOLD` = the current cell (so `identity ≠ position`; `position =
FOLD(history)`), `VERDICT` = the axis decision, `APPEND` = the durable collapse, `CITE` = the legality of
the collapse. A meaning traveler may legally rest at a partial cell — meaning rarely arrives as a
finished corner.

This stays `proposed` for two honest reasons: nothing live reads a coordinate (promotion test: a live
meaning crossing reads/writes/folds one), and the *actual* axes of meaning-decidedness are unknown — the
cube assumes three; a real meaning record may need two, or n, or non-orthogonal axes. The cube's value
is that it offers the **fabric's missing meaning axis** (the work-atom already has `advance`; meaning has
nothing) — but CTA-1 must discover the real axes by forcing one claim to cross, not pick them by elegance.

## 7. Calls to action and sequence

1. **CTA-1 — make the first meaning-bearing ontum real.** Mint one `relation_claim` whose subject is a
   unit of *meaning* (not a work-atom) plus its `consequence_receipt`, so `relation_ledger` scores it.
   *Passes only if:* the subject is meaning-bearing; the claim cites backing; the receipt observes
   consequence rather than self-asserting it; the fold returns `PREDICTIVE` or `TRIVIAL`; `UNTESTED`/
   `UNCLAIMED` stay incomplete; a fabricated meaning primary is refused by `CITE`. This is the smallest
   honest bridge from harness to fabric, and it forces the work/meaning seam to resolve in code.
2. **CTA-2 — extract the derivation skill** (`explore → consequence-map → derive → generate → critique`),
   with a mandatory final stage: run `CITE` on its own derived primaries. It is not real until it
   self-demotes `POSTURE`.
3. **CTA-3 — build the fabric read path** (meaning addressable by consequence/similarity, not only hash).
   *Deferred until CTA-1 produces data.*
4. **CTA-4 — point the NL/generative interface** at the first-ontum schema. *Deferred until the meaning
   center is non-hollow* — do not build an interface over a declared-at-zero core.

**Sequence:** `CTA-1 → CTA-2`, with `CTA-3/CTA-4` gated on CTA-1 producing data. Standing guards: no
compiler before the throat; no shared experience before the fabric; do not promote the cube from lens to
skeleton until a live meaning crossing reads it. And the sequencing correction the review converged on:
do not author a large prior map and defer the test — **mint the first crossing first, then the map is the
posterior it implies.**

## 8. Verdict

```
The harness grammar is real and reduces to five: APPEND · FOLD · HASH-IDENTITY · CITE · VERDICT.
Gallery confirms the grammar under divergent words; POSTURE is a derived governance fold, not a primary.
The first work-projection is derived; the first meaning ontum is not yet real.
The consequence throat is the buildable bridge — assembly of minted parts, proven by refusal.
The cube is not the harness skeleton; it is a proposed fabric prior→posterior lattice, pending CTA-1.
The creole is a legal-move grammar over guarded, cited, folded crossings — not nouns, not verbs.
```

A first ontum is not a filled schema. It is a meaning traveler whose identity is durable, whose state is
folded, whose crossings are cited and verdict-bound, whose ghosts are refused, whose consequence is
scored — and whose place in the fabric may be projected onto a prior→posterior lattice until a live
crossing learns the real axes.
