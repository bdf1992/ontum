# .ai-native/proposals/ — pre-epic captured shapes

The home for **proposals**: a design captured *before* it is an arc — bdo's to
steer, a session's only to propose (D-4). A proposal is the durable record of a
shape worked out in conversation or exploration; it is **PROPOSED** until bdo
names it, at which point it graduates into an `epic.*` (with `pieces`), a
done-line series, or an `outcomes/*` desired-reality — and the proposal stays as
the record of where that arc was born.

Distinct from its neighbours in the records tree:

- `epics/` — **confirmed arcs** (bdo-stamped, with horizon + pieces).
- `outcomes/` — **multi-session desired realities** (evidence-bearing probes).
- `done/` — **frozen session done-lines** ("when met, stop").
- `proposals/` (here) — the **pre-epic shape** that the above graduate *from*.

Rules of this directory:

- **PROPOSED, not minted.** A proposal proposes; it never authorizes its own
  arc. Naming and confirmation are bdo's (D-4).
- **Editable, not frozen.** Unlike a done-line, a proposal deepens as the shape
  sharpens — superseding prose, never a second source of truth (the log stays
  truth; a proposal is a captured map, not a record of fact).
- **A realized proposal stays as history.** When its work lands, the proposal is
  not deleted — it records where the arc was born; it may note "REALIZED → see
  `epic.<id>`" at its head.
- Homed here 2026-06-21 (the root-cleanup): proposals had been accreting loose
  at the repo root, which is reserved for the doctrine and the governance files
  (CLAUDE.md, AGENTS.md, README, CONTRIBUTING). Prose references elsewhere that
  still name a root path are historical — `git log --follow` resolves the move.
