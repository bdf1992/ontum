> **Done when:** A pure read-only fold (`loop/impact.py`, stdlib only, the `loop.energy` / `loop.field` grain) measures **Impact as settling** — the first pan of the assay. It weighs every subject in the whole field (`loop.field.whole_field`) by signals already on the log: **evidence-mass** (records that settle on a subject) and **reach** (distinct arcs it spans), in **water** only (re-derivation, zero new capture). It **calibrates rather than scores absolutely** (bdo: a score is a position between anchors): each subject's settled position is taken between the field's own **floor** and **ceiling** weights, and a field with no spread reads *flat* — no divide, never a fabricated number. It separates the pan honestly into **concentrate** (the heavy residue, ranked), **tailings** (floor-weight — washes through), **unmeasured** (a surfaced gap with no record to weigh — *not* weight-zero; absence is not lightness), and **black sand** (a subject that settles by a *single* signal while the other stays at its floor — the coarse water pan cannot tell these from gold, so it names them and points at the finer mesh a later judged pan will be). Proven by a test: settling tracks the field (a constant / fabricated weigher fails); a gap reads unmeasured, never light; floor==ceiling cannot divide (flat field); calibration is relative to the population (adding a heavier subject shifts the others' positions — a position between anchors, not an absolute); black sand is flagged, not silently ranked as gold; an empty field weighs nothing (absence, not a crash); and over the repo's own whole field the fold weighs real subjects, every one a routed field subject (no ghost). Full suite green; a merge receipt.

> **Non-example:** an absolute impact number from nowhere (mercury / amalgamation — the easy capture that poisons trust; a score with no floor/ceiling anchor); treating an unmeasurable gap as weight-zero tailings (absence faked as lightness); a single-signal settler ranked as gold with no black-sand flag (the coarse mesh pretending to be fine); re-deriving leverage over the Causality probe-set (that is `loop.pressure`'s pan, a different liquid) or the orchestrator's heat/cool (anima's rhythm, `loop.temporal`); capturing any new signal (this pan is water only); or a crash on a flat or empty field.
