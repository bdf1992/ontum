## 1. The correction this records

Session 0003's summary described the loop substrate as "what ontum is
trying to be." **That was a category error, and bdo caught it.** The repo
itself had it right all along:

- Doctrine intent line: *"a method of discipline for building onton
  [now: ontum], not a second system beside it."*
- Phase-2 README: *"These documents describe the wider project the
  workflow engine exists to build."*

The corrected picture, in one sentence each:

- **Ontum** is the fabric: a living system of named, bounded,
  provenance-carrying units of meaning — onta settling into an ontum,
  keystone, jective, cant, drift, etymontoken
  (`docs/phase-2/ontum-evolution.md`).
- **The loop substrate** (`loop/`, `.ai-native/`) is the metabolism that
  keeps such units alive: durability, second-set-of-eyes, receipts,
  ambient control. It is the harness, never the building.
- **bdo's naming for the whole:** a *Reflective Execution Fabric* driven by
  *ambient evidence pressure* — the substrate is the "execution" half,
  the vault is the "reflective" half, and the fabric is what exists when
  meaning-units run on the metabolism.

## 3. The gap the frame names (the build direction)

The sensors built in 0003 read **operational** pressure: queue depth,
staleness, human backlog, inflow. bdo's evidence list is **semantic**
pressure: *name drifted, claim contradicted prior claim, unit lost its
boundary, old assumption became stale.* The doctrine anticipates pieces of
this (I-4 drift-collapse, L1 collision, §13.3 contradiction) but nothing
built can feel it yet — every judge is still a mock.

The vault already holds the instrument: **cant** ("a barycenter that
differs from its centroid is evidence that history happened") is "name
drifted" made measurable; vault §9's warning that cant *silently lies*
without a declared neutral prior is I-8 restated for meaning — a semantic
sensor without an admitted zero reports history where there is none.

**Therefore: making a node real is not just de-mocking — each real node is
the system's first sensor of a given kind of meaning.** L0 real = the
first semantic sensor (does this story serve the owner's value?).
