# Outcome — Causality as the operational knowledge surface, carried by outcome-pressure

*Authored by bdo, 2026-06-13. The desired reality this directory carries
across sessions. Design rationale: `outcome-pressure-fold.proposal.md` (PR
#122). Held in full — decomposed, never shrunk. "Out of scope for a done-line"
does not mean out of scope for this outcome.*

## Maximal outcome (the horizon line)

> **Ontum has a working outcome-pressure system, carried across sessions
> through summon, composed with owner/work pressure, and projected into
> Causality as a persistent, editable, evidence-grounded knowledge surface —
> so a waking session inherits the gap to this outcome and reduces it through
> real increments until the probes resolve, instead of declaring victory
> because one session produced a doc, audit, or partial fold.**

This is one outcome with two faces that need each other: the **pressure
system** (the system can carry an outcome's tension across mortal sessions)
and **Causality** (the surface where that tension becomes a living, typed,
editable, persistent graph you can see, edit, persist, project, and navigate).

## First session-sized move (the first done-line)

The first increment builds **OP1's foundation** and, crucially, makes the
**entire probe-set above visible as continuing pressure** — so the rest of the
outcome does not vanish when the session ends. Its bar is the next numbered
done-line (bdo's wording), and it states explicitly that it is *one increment
toward this maximal outcome, not a replacement for it.*

## What remains after the first move (continuing outcome-pressure)

Everything except OP1's foundation: OP2, OP3, all of M2 (CZ1–CZ4), and M3
(OUT1, OUT2). These are **not "out of scope for the goal"** — they are the
outcome, simply *not completed by the first done-line*. Once OP1 exists, they
are no longer prose in a file bdo holds in his head; they are **unresolved
probes the fold surfaces at every wake**, ranked by phase and leverage, until
they resolve. That is the system carrying the outcome across sessions — the
point of the whole thing.
