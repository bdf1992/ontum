## Cube-Coil Reader Key

```text
A clean technical learning-card diagram titled "Cube-Coil Reader Key".
White background, crisp vector style, subtle pencil-notes texture.

Center: a transparent cube standing on one body diagonal, black corner at bottom,
white corner at top, gray glowing point at the center. Label the cube roles:
"1 center = observer", "6 faces = senses", "8 vertices = primitives",
"12 edges = bindings".

Around the vertical body diagonal, draw a helical coil/solenoid path passing through
the gray center neck, with small notes: "winding = spin",
"neck crossing = inversion / anti", "stored = energy".

Around the cube, three nested soft boundary shells:
inner: "PINNED — computed / grounded"
middle: "OPEN — unresolved / testable"
outer halo: "MYTHOS — read-only / orienting / never computed".

Caption: "Read: PLACE → FIELD → PATH → STATUS".
Minimal colors: black, white, gray, with RGB/CMY accents only on the cube vertices.
High compression, readable at small size, educational infographic style.
```
