# Operations — the foundational primitives (v0)

The operations the binary structure and math require: the **five ways parts combine,
filter, scale, split, or resolve** (the Color Operations card). Every path's goal is to
**reach gray** — the neutral, resolved, coherent whole (gray = color center, charge 0,
the conserved singlet, the cube center / observer).

## The five operations

| # | operation | symbol | does | math | reduces to (binary) |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| 01 | **Additive** | `+` | parts accumulate | a + b | OR / rail-sum |
| 02 | **Subtractive** | `−` | parts remove / filter | a − b | AND-NOT / mask |
| 03 | **Multiply** | `×` | parts scale / interact | a × b | AND / tensor ⊗ |
| 04 | **Divisive** | `÷` | parts split / ratio / normalize | a ÷ b | shift / partition |
| 05 | **Resolute** | `⚖` | opposites balance into a valid whole | a ⊕ ā → 0 | XOR → neutral |

- **Reach gray = the resolved neutral whole.** Gray is **not** emptiness or the average
  — it is the point where the concept **holds under all relevant tests.** So
  **Resolute = the verdict / gate**: reconcile opposites until the whole is coherent
  (the §10 validity check; the color-neutral singlet; the conserved sum → 0).
- **Order matters** — each operation shapes the path; intermediate results are valid
  checkpoints (the grades). **Balance > quantity; completeness > size.**

## The binary primitives underneath

The color operations reduce to bit / dual-rail operations — what the **binary
structure** actually requires:

- **NOT / invert** — the complement; the **Prime** (chiral flip through the neck).
- **XOR / toggle** — the sign-bit flip; **Anti** (charge conjugation; signed flip).
- **AND · OR** — mask / accumulate (Subtractive · Additive).
- **dual-rail combine** — `(+rail, −rail) →` the charge state (Null / + / − / +−).
- **shift / partition** — Divisive (split a bound into modes — the harmonics).
- **compose · inverse · identity** — the group operations (the cube's symmetries:
  duality C₂ · triality C₃).

## What this gives the math

- arithmetic (`+ − × ÷`) over the fields (charge / color / spin),
- **Resolute** (`⚖`) = conservation / neutralization → the valid whole (singlet, gray),
- all grounded in binary (the rails) — so every operation is **computable and
  enumerable** (the bounded-wave principle).

**Reach-gray checklist (the operating procedure):** identify parts → choose operation →
check intermediate result → verify balance (roles, opposites, constraints) → resolve to
gray (confirm coherence). *Operate with intention.*
